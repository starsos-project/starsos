# Code of Conduct

## Summary

Be kind. Assume good faith. Disagree with substance, not snark.

## In detail

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/). The short version:

- **Welcome everyone**, regardless of background, identity, or experience level
- **Use inclusive language** — pronouns, accessible explanations, no in-group jokes that exclude
- **Critique ideas, not people** — focus on the substance of what's being discussed
- **Accept feedback gracefully** — both giving and receiving
- **Take responsibility** for mistakes and learn from them

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported confidentially to **conduct@starsos.org**. Reports are reviewed within 7 days. We may warn, mute, or ban participants who repeatedly or egregiously violate this code.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces: the repository, issues, pull requests, discussions, Discord, social media accounts, and any in-person or virtual events.
